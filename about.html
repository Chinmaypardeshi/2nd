Our Story

Discover the journey behind our authentic Maharashtrian flavors

Welcome to Maharashtrian Thali

Founded in 2010, Maharashtrian Thali was born out of a deep love for traditional Maharashtrian cuisine. Our founder, Chef Rajesh Patil, wanted to bring the authentic flavors of Maharashtra to food lovers everywhere.

What started as a small family restaurant has now grown into one of the most beloved Maharashtrian dining destinations, known for its commitment to quality, authenticity, and warm hospitality.

Our Mission

At Maharashtrian Thali, our mission is simple: to serve authentic Maharashtrian cuisine made with the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. We believe in preserving the rich culinary heritage of Maharashtra while adding our own modern touch.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible and work closely with farmers and suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.
